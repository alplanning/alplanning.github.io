Anton Lang Planning Services Limited

Benefits & Testimonials

The main benefit from employing Anton Lang Planning Services Limited is that not only do you instruct a Chartered Town Planner but you also gain from his skills as a fully academically-qualified, but unregistered Barrister; having been admitted as a member of the Honourable Society of the Inner Temple.

Anton Lang Planning Services Limited is a private limited company incorporated in 2008 under the Companies Act 1985 and registered with Companies House.

The company carries full Professional Indemnity Insurance and is Value Added Tax (VAT) registered.

Additional benefits are:

  • a single, experienced and qualified professional dealing with all aspects of every job;
  • the advanced advocacy, drafting and negotiation skills of a trained barrister (unregistered and not offering legal services);
  • sympathy and customer awareness with regards to costs and budgets;
  • near-immediate responses to all communications;
  • inside-knowledge from time spent at various local authorities, quango and consultancies;
  • dedication, motivation and persistence (as previous clients attest, see below).

Anton Lang remains the only planning professional to be nominated and twice short-listed for the Young Planner of the Year Award (in the inaugural year, 2000 and also in 2005).

In 2018, Anton was asked to contribute to a live and unscripted BBC Radio 4 debate on Listed Buildings: England now has 400,000 listed buildings. Too many or not enough?. You can hear him promote a more progressive and pro-development stance on the Front Row arts & culture programme here (first aired on 29 August 2018).
